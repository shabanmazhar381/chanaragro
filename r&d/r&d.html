    Research & Development


    The ongoing commitment to the concept of human space cannot be separate from the support of the department of research and development. Efforts of Chanar Agro in R&D are supported by well-developed infrastructure facilities of land, laboratory and qualified experienced man power. We have well qualified experienced personnel in R&D. Our R&D staff has excellent exposure to update research and has undergone various training programs.

    Objective

      To provide genetically pure seeds
      To develop early maturing varieties/Hybrids
      To develop Varieties/Hybrids that flourish better in changing atmosphere.
      To develop products that is resistant and tolerant to Biotic stresses (ever evolving diseases and insects) and unusual temperatures.
